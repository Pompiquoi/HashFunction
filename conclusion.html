Conclusion

Hash functions remain a cornerstone of modern cryptography. However, their vulnerabilities to collision and pre-image attacks highlight the need for constant vigilance and improvement. Algorithms like SHA-3 offer hope for more secure systems, but the emergence of quantum computing presents new challenges that must be addressed proactively.
